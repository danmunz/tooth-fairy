Project Description

This is a private, family-operated SMS service that allows a child to exchange text messages with an AI-powered "Tooth Fairy" character. This is a personal project created by a parent/guardian to provide a magical, educational experience for their child.

Opt-In Process

This service is NOT open to the public. Access is restricted to:

  • The child for whom this service was created
  • Parent(s)/legal guardian(s) who set up and maintain the service
  • Other family members explicitly authorized by the parents

Consent is provided as follows:

  • For the child: Explicit verbal consent from parent/legal guardian, who maintains full control and oversight of the service
  • For parents/guardians: Self-enrollment (they created and operate the service)
  • For other family members: Explicit verbal consent obtained by the parent/guardian

Scope and Use

  • Volume: Low volume, conversational messaging (estimated 50-100 messages per month)
  • Purpose: Entertainment, education, and fostering imagination
  • Participants: Limited to immediate family members only
  • Content: Family-friendly, age-appropriate conversational responses

Privacy and Safety

  • Only pre-authorized phone numbers can interact with the service
  • All conversations are monitored by parents/guardians
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Service can be discontinued at any time by the parent/guardian
  • All participants can opt-out by simply stopping texting the number

Contact

This is a private family service. For questions or concerns, participants should contact the parent/guardian who operates this service directly.

Parental Consent Statement

  • I have obtained appropriate consent for all participants
  • All participants are members of my immediate family
  • I maintain full control and oversight of all messaging
  • This service is for personal, non-commercial family use only
